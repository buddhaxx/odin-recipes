Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

A deliciously creamy garlic parmesan pasta is what everyone craves when it comes to comfort food! It's really hard to resist the cheesy, garlic cream slathered over noodles no matter the time of year. With that being said, let us waste no more time - here is the recipe!

Ingredients:

To start this wonderful treat, first we need to gather ingredients! Here is a list of what we need:

  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, finely grated
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves fresh garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 8 oz. angel hair pasta
  • Fresh parsley to garnish

Now that we've got that all covered, let's put it all together!

Directions

  1. Melt 2 tbsp of butter over medium heat in a large skillet.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the chicken broth. Slowly add the milk, stirring as you do so. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add the pasta noodles. You can break in half or let them heat until soft.
  5. Let the pasta simmer for 4-5- minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure the noodles don't stick together. Reduce heat to low.
  6. Add 1 tablespoon of butter to warm pasta and toss to coat.
  7. Gradually sprinkle in parmesan as you're stirring until it is smooth and combined. Note: The sauce will be thin at first, but it will thicken upon standing.
  8. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve!

For even more awesome flavor, be sure to serve with a tasteful serving of garlic bread with cheese! This recipe is good for four servings, so be sure to bring your appetite when you make this recipe!
