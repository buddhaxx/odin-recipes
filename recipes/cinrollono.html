Cinnamon Roll Overnight Oats

Cinnamon rolls, who doesn't love them?! Now, we also have the option to have cinnamon rolls in the form of Overnight oats in a very short and easy to follow recipe. With a short ingredients list and simple put-together, this easy, overnight treat is sure to make any one's morning a delight!

Ingredients:

To start this wonderful treat, first we need to gather ingrediets! Here is a list of what we need:

  • 1 1/2 cups of old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 2 1/2 cups of unsweetened, nondairy milk such as almond or coconut
  • 6 teaspoons of light brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Now that we've got that all covered, let's put it all together!

Directions

  1. Stir all of the ingredients thoroughly into a large bowl.
  2. Divide it among five 8 oz. mason jars.
  3. Screw on the lids and refrigerate overnight.

Once that is all taken care of, all that is left is to wake up, crack open a jar and enjoy!
